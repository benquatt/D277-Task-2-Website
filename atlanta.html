Welcome to Atlanta!

Atlanta is the capital city of Georgia. It is a hustling hub for many industries, and is home to the headquarters for major international corporations, such as Coca-Cola and Home Depot. The last United States-hosted Olympics were held in Atlanta in 1996.

Atlanta Facts:

  • The population of Atlanta is approximately 500,000 as of 2022
  • The city of Atlanta was founded on December 29, 1847
  • The city is centrally located within the state of Georgia
  • The city is classified as urban
  • The average income for citizens of Atlanta is $45,956, which is higher than the average state income of $35,753
