Welcome to Savannah!

Savannah is one of the most well-known cities in Georgia.

Savannah Facts:

  • The population of Savannah is approximately 150,000 as of 2022
  • The city of Savannah was founded in 1733
  • The city is located on the eastern coast of Georgia near the South Carolina border
  • The city is classified as urban
  • The average income for citizens of Savannah is $28,630, which is lower than the average state income of $35,753
